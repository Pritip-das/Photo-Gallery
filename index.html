Photo Gallery

Lion

Lions are large, social cats known as "king of the jungle," living in prides and primarily found in Africa.

Tiger

Tigers are large, powerful predators with distinctive orange coats and black stripes, native to Asia and critically endangered.

elephant

Elephants are large, intelligent mammals with trunks, known for their social structure, memory, and roles in ecosystems across Africa and Asia.

Fox

Foxes are small, omnivorous mammals known for their bushy tails, pointed ears, and adaptability to various environments.

squirrel

Squirrels are small, agile rodents known for their bushy tails and habit of hoarding nuts for winter.

flamingo

Flamingos are wading birds known for their pink feathers, long legs, and unique feeding habits involving filter-feeding in shallow waters.

bear

Bears are large mammals found in forests and mountains, known for their strength, diverse diets, and hibernation habits.

Eagle

Eagles are large, powerful birds of prey known for their keen eyesight, strong talons, and majestic flight.
